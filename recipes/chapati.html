Chapati recipe

Home

Description

Chapati is also another food that people love and prefer

Ingredients

  • 1kg wheat flour
  • 2 Tablespoon Margarine
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 Cups warm water
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar and salt. Add Margarine, mix with hands to form crumbs. You can add a little bit of sugar to bring a savoury taste
  2. Pour water bit by bit as you knead. Knead at least at least 5 minutes until the dough is soft to cook very soft chapatis
  3. Finally dough should be firm but soft and not sticky. Cover with a damp cloth/cling film/plastic polythene bag for 30 minutes
  4. After 30 minutes, knead again and cut into balls
  5. Roll out the balls in a circular shape with a rolling pin, and brush with oil
  6. Roll the balls out again forming a circle
  7. In a medium heat, place your skillet. Let it warm up. Then put your chapati on top. Fry both sides until brown. Once cooked remove and place it in a container and cover
  8. Serve with your favourite stew or even tea. Enjoy
