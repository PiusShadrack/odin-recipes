Pilau Recipe

Home

Description

Pilau is a Kenyan made food that is cooked with rice and some ingredients

It is regarded as one of the sweetest foods in Kenya

Ingredients

  • 1/2kg rice
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Cooking oil
  • Pilau masala

Steps

  1. Chop the garlic into small sized pieces as well as the onion
  2. Put them into a sufuria and add them three teaspoonful of cooking oil
  3. Mix the onions with some pilau masala wait for aroun 2 minutes
  4. Add 1kg white rice into the boiling mixture and stir
  5. Finally add 2 litres of water in the mixture and give it around 15 minutes to boil and also for the water to dry
  6. Serve the ready food
